About Me

Passionate GIS, Remote Sensing & GeoAI Researcher

I am Abu Naser Sajib, an undergraduate researcher from the Department of Geography and Environment, Jagannath University, Dhaka, Bangladesh. My research integrates Geographic Information Systems (GIS), Remote Sensing, GeoAI, Explainable Artificial Intelligence, and Spatial Machine Learning to address environmental challenges, disaster risk reduction, urban sustainability, and climate resilience.

My long-term goal is to contribute to cutting-edge geospatial research through advanced Earth Observation, AI-driven environmental modelling, and interdisciplinary spatial analytics.
